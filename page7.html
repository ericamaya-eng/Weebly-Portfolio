About Eric Amaya
Eric Amaya’s work is characterized by a cartoony and exaggerated style, complemented by quick representational pieces depicting landscapes and still life.

His chosen mediums include graphite-based sketch line art, conté and charcoal line art, and oil paints. He also occasionally explores digital media and has a growing interest in 3D graphics and music production.

Amaya’s primary motivation stems from a desire to bring his ideas to life on paper. His artwork is vibrant and pop-inspired, often reflecting the aesthetic and energy of video games, particularly those in the indie scene and titles geared toward younger audiences aged 6 to 12. In contrast, his representational works draw influence from the Impressionist movement.

Amaya takes pride in his ability to create fun, dynamic caricatures that resonate with a wide audience, as well as essential representational artworks that connect with an even broader range of viewers.
