Logo
My design process for this logo was to make something bubbly and fun, but with a cool, hard edge to match my Y2K aesthetic cartoons. I'd say the messy texture the word has, while still adhering to traditional bubble word formatting, really gets that idea of fun but cool across. It definitely conveys my identity as an artist with the way I draw eyes being front and center, and it showcases the cool texture my line art has. However, it also represents how I draw everything round.
